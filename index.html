Welcome to the Digital Museums Winter Semester 19⁄20 Seminar Project Page

Here you can find a comprehensive literature review on the topic of Digital Museums, Digitasation of Museums, and Humanities research. You can also contribute to the project.

Your contribution is indeed highly appreciated through:

  • German support for the page.

  • Support development of the Project:
